Welcome

AMS 360 is a full-service CNC and CAD/CAM training provider in Windsor, Ontario, Canada. We have combined a team of experts from various manufacturing sectors to form a complete and flexible customer training experience. All services are available during typical business hours, afterhours, and on weekends; an alternative option for night shift personnel, and those whom would rather work their usual day shift and train in the evenings.

Our services include product training, optimization of the programming process, machine tool data creation, and post processor builds.

Our team of Applications Engineers are available to assess your current manufacturing solutions; and provide tailored, leading-edge CAM training. We have a proven curriculum and are ready to help you achieve the full potential from your digital manufacturing solution.
