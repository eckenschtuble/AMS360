WHO ARE WE?

AMS 360 is a full-service CNC and CAD/CAM training provider in Windsor, Ontario, Canada. We have combined a team of experts from various manufacturing sectors to form a complete, customizable training curriculum. Our services include product training, optimization of the programming process (macro/VbScript), machine tool data creation for simulation, and post processor builds. AMS 360 was recently incorporated by Robert Kobielski.

Robert Kobielski is a CAD/CAM consultant and trainer with nearly 20 years of manufacturing and teaching experience. He is a proud member of faculty at St. Clair College, responsible for coordinating the Mechanical Technician CAD/CAM program. Alongside this Robert is a volunteer for SKILLS ONTARIO and F.I.R.S.T.© robotics competitions; aiming to help motivate the next generation of skilled tradespeople towards STEM related careers in Manufacturing. His credentials include 429A General Machinist Apprenticeship and Qualification (2001), and Ontario College of Teachers Certification (2009).

In February of 2013 Robert joined Delcam International’s team as an Applications Engineer (since acquired by Autodesk® in 2014). A few notable duties in this role included training, support, and product demonstration of high-end CAD, CAM, and metrology solutions. Employment with Autodesk/Delcam offered Robert fantastic networking and travel opportunities, and cultivated a comprehensive understanding of flagship products like PowerMILL and PowerSHAPE.

Robert and his team of Applications Engineers are eager to assess your current manufacturing solutions; and provide tailored, leading-edge CAM training. We have a proven curriculum and are ready to help you achieve the full potential from your digital manufacturing solution.
