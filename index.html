Welcome!

Title

I am a CAD/CAM consultant and trainer with over 18 years of manufacturing and teaching experience. I am proud to be the coordinator of the Mechanical Technician CAD/CAM program at St. Clair College, and a volunteer for SKILLS ONTARIO and F.I.R.S.T.© robotics competitions. My credentials include 429A General Machinist Apprenticeship and Qualification (2001), and Ontario College of Teachers Certification (2009).

In February of 2013 I had the pleasure of joining Delcam International’s team as an Applications Engineer (since acquired by Autodesk® in 2014). In this role I was responsible for training, support, and product demonstration of high-end CAD, CAM, and metrology solutions. This role offered me fantastic networking and travel opportunities, and provided me with a comprehensive understanding of the company’s flagship products like PowerMILL and PowerSHAPE.

As director of AMS 360 I strive to connect businesses with full circle, leading-edge CAM training. I have a proven curriculum and I am ready to help you achieve the full potential from your software.
