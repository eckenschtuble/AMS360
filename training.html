Training Options

AMS 360 Inc. can provide your company with complete training and consultation services, tailored to fit your needs. All services are available during typical business hours, afterhours, and on weekends.

Evening classes are typically scheduled in four-hour blocks. Below are the training and service options for core products of the digital manufacturing group: PowerMILL® PowerSHAPE® PowerINSPECT® FeatureCAM®

Classroom Training

• Proven curriculum delivered by a certified trainer
• Available to individuals or small groups (minimum class size required)
• Days / evenings / weekends
• 3 axis and/or 5 axis

On-site / Off-site Training

• Training as above dedicated to only your trainees
• Custom models, topics, and/or specialty applications available (must be coordinated in advance)

Machine-Tool-Data Service

• Create or customize your machine tool simulation data for complete toolpath verification

Post-Processor Service

• Create or customize your post processor
• 3, 4, or 5 axis development available

Optimization / Customization / Advanced Macro / VbScript Service

• Customize toolbar ribbons and commands
• Automate repetitive manual programming sequences
• Construct powerful computing sequences with error-handling and subroutines
