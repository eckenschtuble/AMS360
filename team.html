Meet our team

JEFF STELLING

Post Development / Applications Engineer

Jeff leads our post development team, and provides technical support and training for Autodesk’s full portfolio of CAD/CAM software. Jeff has worked in the manufacturing industry for 20 years, and has extensive knowledge in the tool & die, and mold tool industries. He began his career as a tool and die maker, followed by many years as a machinist/programmer of 3, 4, and 5-axis CNC machines.

MIKE BELANGER

Business Development Manager

Mike brings an additional 20 years of experience to the team. In his first 15 years he gained great experience as a senior machinist using PowerMill on high-end 5-axis machines, and training new recruits. Then hired by Delcam (acquired by Autodesk), Mike spent over 5 years working with the Advanced Manufacturing technical support and implementation teams, specializing in PowerMill, PowerShape, Electrode Solution, and more. Mike carries with him a wide product and skills breadth, and looks forward to helping companies grow their business and optimize their digital manufacturing solution.

MICHAEL SPADOTTO

Education Engagement and Marketing Coordinator

Michael specializes in education engagement and market strategy. He manages short and long term relationships with education institutions, and produces marketing material for both industry and vocational learning institutes.

ROBERT KOBIELSKI

Director of Operations

Robert Kobielski is a CAD/CAM consultant and educator with 20 years of manufacturing and teaching experience. His credentials include 429A General Machinist Apprenticeship and Qualification, and Ontario College of Teachers Certification.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT

We have a contract team of 4 applications engineers deeply versed in a variety of products. Each member of our team is CAM specialized, and an authorized instructor in their respective discipline.
